## VoxPen Is Here

We're excited to announce that VoxPen is officially live! It's an AI-powered voice input tool built with privacy at its core, available on Android, Windows, and Linux.

### Why VoxPen?

Most voice input tools require a monthly subscription, and your voice data passes through the service provider's servers. VoxPen uses a **BYOK (Bring Your Own Key)** architecture — you get your own API key, and your voice goes directly to the AI provider with no intermediary.

### Key Features

- **Lightning Fast**: Groq Whisper speech recognition — results in 0.5 seconds
- **Smart Refinement**: AI removes filler words, fixes grammar, adds punctuation
- **Multilingual**: 11 languages with auto-detection and mixed-language support
- **One-Time Purchase**: No monthly fees — buy once, use forever
